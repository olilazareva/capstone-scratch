Bali boards

Bali boards website was created for a same name local balinese surfboard manufacturer with two stores in Kuta and Canggu, where both beginners and pro surfers can choose a perfect board at a fair price.

Along with unbeatable range of surfboards they also create custom boards, offer trade ins, 30-day guarantee and free delivery.
